About

Greenfield to production. Discovery to delivery. Repeat.

I run product from blank page to shipped - owning strategy, ways of working, and delivery in regulated financial services environments where complexity is the default. Two greenfield platforms in production in under five months each. A squad of ten. The full lifecycle: research, prioritisation, spec, build, QA, go-live, and the iteration that follows.

I built and rolled out an AI-assisted operating model across my organisation - not a tool rollout, a fundamental redesign of how we run discovery, write requirements, do QA, and make decisions. The result was a measurable step-change in delivery speed and defect rates. I think of AI as a process design problem, not a tooling one.

I started as a Product Engineer, so I bring real technical depth to architecture calls, trade-off conversations, and engineering partnerships. I know what I'm asking for when I ask for it - and I can tell when something is genuinely hard versus when it just needs a clearer brief.
